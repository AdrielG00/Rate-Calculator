NGIC – Single Use
• National General Ins phone #: 800-325-1088
• Services: ERS towing, jump start, winch out, lockout, fuel delivery, tire services
NGIC Single Use is a reimbursement program for RV breakdowns. The insured will pay out of pocket before dispatch. They will need to submit the receipt to National General Insurance for reimbursment evaluation.
o If customer asks policy / coverage questions: Redirect customer to National General Ins 800-325-1088
o If customer is requesting service on a light duty vehicle: Redirect customer to National General Ins 800-325-1088 and follow the appropriate prompts
DO NOT IMFORM MEMBER THEY NEED TO CALL NATIONAL GENERAL FOR A CLAIM # on RV BREAKDOWNS UNLESS THEY ARE REFUSING TO PAY AND YOU HAVE TRIED TO OVERCOME THE OBJECTION BY EXPLAINING REIMBURMENT PROCESS.

!!! Collect payment before dispatch!!!
- Service call – Add $75 to vendors quote
- MD / HD tow – Add $150 to vendors quote
o If member is requesting a “mobile mechanic”
o Claim #: Call National General to verify coverage
o Note move snapshot with adjuster’s name, phone # and email address
o Afterhours – Proceed as fully covered if unable to reach NGIC and true emergency roadside event. Email leads@transitpros.com for appropriate follow up during business hours
o No claim #: See “NGIC – Single Use” procedures
o Inform member we do not set that service up directly and do not know if that is included in their reimbursment policy
o Google mechanics local to the customer if the do not have access – provide customer with Co name and number
o Advise member to save their receipt from the provider of their choice to submit for reimbursement consideration with National General.
