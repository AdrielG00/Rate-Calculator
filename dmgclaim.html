Damage Claim Process

If you get a call from a customer, vendor or client regarding a complaint or damage claim it is VITAL that you do NOT accept any responsibility for the damage and do not admit any fault. Keep things very black and white do not leave grey area for them to misinterpret what you are saying to them. Be clear, get all the details and assure them someone will be in contact with them soon on how to proceed. You MUST email damage@transitpros.com immediately.

A few things you will need to ask:
 Name
 Email address- asking for their email address will speed up our initial claims process.
 Phone number

Do they have photos of the damage and would they prefer to:
o Email- damage@transitpros.com (preferred)
o Text- 816-698-6562

Make sure they list the move number with the photos that they send
Let them know that you will send it to our claims department and a representative will be in contact with them within 1 business day.

Final Steps:
Notate the move with the details- email that through the system to damage@transitpros.com be sure to put in if the photos will be emailed or texted.
