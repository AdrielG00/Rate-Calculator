Client Breakdown:
 Masters Transportation rents/leases shuttle busses and school busses to several companies, including Military Bases and Government agencies. Some service requests may come from Masters Transportation directly - but they may also come in from customers who are leasing/renting a unit and are in need of assistance. These call ins must be verified for authorization to proceed from client before dispatching a provider. In order to get authorization - it is imperative that we get the Z# for the unit (last 6 digits of the vin) for Masters to confirm this unit belongs to them and authorize the service request.
