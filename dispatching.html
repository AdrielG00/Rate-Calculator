Dispatching:


• Make the initial offer & negotiate!
o “The job pays…”
o “How close to ${total} van we get on this one?”
o “Will $XXX.XX get this job done?”


Sample Phrases


“This is ________ with Transit Pros. Do you have anyone available to tow a _________ from ________ to ___________?”
“How quick can you get to this?”, “What is your ETA?”
“We have this going ____ miles, will $xxx.xx get that done?”
“How close to $xxx.xxx, can you get on this?”, “Can you meet me in this middle at $xxx.xx?”


What if a vendor can't help?

If a vendor cannot help, ask who they recommend to help.
If the delivery is a shop - call the shop for a vendor recommendation
Utilize resources if unable to locate a vendor from TP list - geo code, google, NTTS
Check with your team lead for recommendations
